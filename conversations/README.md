# Adding Conversation Context

Drop your exported chat files here to give your agents more context.

## Supported formats

- DeepSeek export (conversations.json from deepseek_data.zip)
- ChatGPT export (conversations.json from your data export)
- Plain text files (.txt) with recipe Q&A

## How it helps

The more recipe conversations you add, the more the agent understands your family's
specific variations — "my mom's version", regional adaptations, substitutions for
hard-to-find ingredients.

## Note on privacy

These files stay on your machine. They are never uploaded anywhere.
Only your Claude API calls leave your device.
